FAQ

Q

Do I have to publish a post recommended by DoubleViz?

No. Just skip the recommendations you don’t like. You have full control of what to post on your own blog.

Q

How exactly does the AI find the matching blogs for me?

DoubleViz first analyzes your content using the Deep Learning (DL) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) so to understand your writing styles, interested subjects, and other factors. It then recommends articles with similar style and/or related subjects that can enrich your blog. Similarly, it posts your articles on other blogs having the right audience to create genuine reads for you.

Q

Do I have control over the AI?

Yes. By default, DoubleViz saves your time by automating things. But you can opt into manual controls. For example, you can set a blacklist of words (such as the names of your competitors) to prevent the containing posts from being recommended. You can decide whether to publish a recommend guest post on your blog or not. You can also tell DoubeViz when to make recommendations such that it fits your schedule.

Q

Does DoubleViz do anything good to SEO?

Probably. A search engine may crawl the blog pages that guest-post your articles and link back to your blog. This creates positive effects on SEO. However, please don’t take DoubleViz as an SEO tool since it is not optimized for creating backlinks. DoubleViz is optimized for increasing genuine reads.
